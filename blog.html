Best Laptop in 2022

Title description, Oct 10, 2022

Abhishek Tech Welcomes, all of you to my world of blog. Some text about this blog entry. To help you buy the best laptop of 2022

Here are some factors to consider:

Battery life varies widely from one laptop to another. If you frequently need to use your laptop away from a power source, you'll want a computer that offers more hours of use between charges. More professionals are working remotely than ever before, so battery life and portability are more relevant than ever.

Smaller laptops with 11-inch or 13-inch screens are usually lighter and more portable, suitable for people who must stay "on the move" for work. Larger laptops with 15-inch or 17-inch screens are better for multimedia and creative applications. This is because creatives often have two large screens to run multiple applications at a time. With these factors in mind, the best laptop size will depend on your exact needs.

In 2021, touch screen laptops may be an upgrade worth making. It's better for users that want a more intuitive experience, and it's also great for creatives. For example, a touch screen can make it easier for illustrators and designers to draw, take notes, or save space in the office. A 4K laptop will provide a higher, more superior viewing experience. These laptops with much higher resolutions give a more immersive experience, whether working, gaming, or streaming.

For users who spend more time on their laptops, ergonomic keyboards are ideal. Laptops are not only specifically just for work or entertainment purposes – and there's a good chance that you use your computer for more than one reason.

Data Science

Title description, Oct 12, 2022

Abhishek Tech Welcomes, all of you to my world of blog. Data science combines math and statistics, specialized programming, advanced analytics, artificial intelligence (AI), and machine learning with specific subject matter expertise to uncover actionable insights hidden in an organization’s data. These insights can be used to guide decision making and strategic planning.

The accelerating volume of data sources, and subsequently data, has made data science is one of the fastest growing field across every industry. As a result, it is no surprise that the role of the data scientist was dubbed the “sexiest job of the 21st century” by Harvard Business Review (link resides outside of IBM). Organizations are increasingly reliant on them to interpret data and provide actionable recommendations to improve business outcomes.

The data science lifecycle involves various roles, tools, and processes, which enables analysts to glean actionable insights.
